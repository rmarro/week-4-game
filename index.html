Crystal Collector

You will be given a target number and 4 gems, which each hide a random number between 1-12. When you click a gem, it will increase your total by that hidden number.

Your goal is to get your total number to match the target number by discovering each gem's value and increasing your total strategically

If your total matches the target, you win. If your total goes over the target, you lose.

Target numbers and gem values change at the start of each round.
